Complete Landscaping Services

Professional landscape maintenance, design, and installation services for residential and commercial properties

Mowing

Mowing will be performed as needed throughout the duration of the contract. All mover heights will be adjusted accordingly to accommodate the needs. All debris will be removed from the lawn prior to mowing. Trimming will be performed around all obstacles and along any spaces the mower(s) are unable to reach.

Edging

Edging will be performed bi-weekly. Edging will maintain a sharp, professionally tailored appearance along all hard surfaces on the property.

Weed Control

A chemical post-emergent herbicide is applied to control grass, and weed growth in and around beds, sidewalks, curbs, and any other structures needing weed control. Hand weeding will be done where chemical herbicides are not recommended for use.

Fertilization and Weed Control

Each fertilization and weed control application will be customized to meet the specific needs of turf. Fertilizations with broadleaf weed control over the length of your contract. Applications will be applied as needed.

Turf Aeration

We will mechanically pull-out core soil plugs from your turf to reduce soil compaction. Reduction of soil compaction will enhance the amounts of nutrients, moister, and oxygen available to the turf roots. Insuring for a better-quality turf.

Debris Policing

Weekly policing of the property during the growing season to ensure a neat and professional appearance.

Spring Clean Up

Includes removal of winter debris that have collected throughout the property, the cutting of ornamental grasses and dormant perennials.

Fall Clean Up

Will consist of removing leaves, sticks, and other debris from the property.

Spring Start Up

Will include turning on the water, activation, and check of the sprinkler system. If repairs are necessary

Irrigation Checks

Will be performed and consist of inspecting sprinkler zones, drip zones, and watering frequency. Irrigation season runs April through October.

Irrigation Winterization

Irrigation systems will be shut down, which will include shutting off the water to the system, draining all backflow devices, and blowing out all irrigation lines.
